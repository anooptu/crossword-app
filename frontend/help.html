How to Play

Objective

Solve easy cricket clues covering batting, bowling, scoring, and field play.

Controls

  • Click on a cell to select it.
  • Type a letter to fill in the selected cell. The selection will automatically move to the next cell in the word.
  • Use the arrow keys to navigate the grid.
  • Click on a clue in the list to highlight the corresponding word on the grid.

Scoring

  • You get 10 points for each correctly solved cricket clue.
  • Your total score is the sum of the points for all solved words.

Game Rule

  • Each participant gets one official attempt.
  • After submission, your score is final and the leaderboard is shown.
